Favorez App

When the pandemic came I see an opportunity to help other people to make an extra income doing home delivery. This application helps to connect people that need to buy something from local stores with delivery mans.

Available on the Google Play Store

Features

  • The client can check in real time the location of his order.
  • The delivery man receives exact instructions to deliver the order.
  • The user can decide to be delivery man or just client so you can switch between client profile or delivery man anytime.
  • You can start adding products directly from one of the business registered in the Favorez Business App

Technical features

  • Architectural pattern MVVM
  • Uses Google Maps and directions API
  • Consume web services from AWS
  • Uses Firebase
    • Authentication
    • Cloud messaging
    • Firestore
    • Storage
    • Cloud functions
    • Crashlytics
  • Reactive extensions
    • RxJava and RxAndroid
  • SQLite for database
  • CameraX
  • Java and Kotlin
  • Material Design
  • Custom Animations
  • JUnit, espresso and mockito for testing

Favorez for Business

I have created this application to help the local businesses to offer home delivery for his clients, by paying low or no commissions. The information that the business register here appears in the Favorez App for clients. In this application the business can manage his information, his business hours and register his products including a photo, name, description and his availability.
